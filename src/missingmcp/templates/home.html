How it works

Step 1

Pick a connector

Choose an app from the list and copy its server URL.

Step 2

Add it to Claude

Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector. Works on phone, desktop, and web.

Step 3

Sign in once

Claude opens a sign-in page — log in with your own account. Your password is used once and never stored.

Step 4

Start asking

Claude picks up the tools automatically. “How did I sleep this week?” just works.

Security & trust

You’re signing in with real credentials — here’s exactly how they’re handled.

Password: used once, never stored

Your password signs you in to the service and is immediately discarded. Only the resulting session tokens are kept — encrypted with AES-256-GCM.

Standard OAuth 2.1

Claude never sees your credentials. It gets its own revocable token, over the same OAuth + PKCE flow that banks and APIs use.

Your data passes through, nothing sticks

Your health and shopping data flows from the service to Claude on demand. The gateway stores none of it.
