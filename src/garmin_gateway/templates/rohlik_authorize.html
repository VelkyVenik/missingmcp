Connect your Rohlík account

Sign in with your Rohlík credentials to connect your account to Claude.

What happens to your data
  • Rohlík's MCP server requires your email and password with every request, so this gateway stores both, encrypted (AES-256-GCM) on this server.
  • Your credentials never appear in logs and are sent only to Rohlík.
  • Your groceries & order data is passed through on demand and is not stored by this gateway.
  • Your credentials are never shared with Claude / Anthropic.
