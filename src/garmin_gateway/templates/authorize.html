Connect your Garmin account

Sign in with your Garmin Connect credentials to connect your account to Claude.

What happens to your data
  • Your password is used once to sign in to Garmin and is never stored — it's discarded as soon as you're authenticated.
  • Only the resulting Garmin session tokens are kept, encrypted (AES-256-GCM) on this server, so it can reach your Garmin data on your behalf.
  • Your Garmin health & activity data is passed through on demand and is not stored by this gateway.
  • Your credentials are never shared with Claude / Anthropic.
